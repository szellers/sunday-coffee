What is Patio Coffee Club?

Every Sunday morning, the back patio transforms into a little neighborhood coffee spot. We brew something special, pull up some chairs, and enjoy the quiet hours before the week kicks in.

Whether you're a coffee nerd who wants to geek out on brew methods or just looking for a slow Sunday morning with good people — you're welcome here.

No reservations. No fuss. Just show up.
